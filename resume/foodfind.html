FoodFind Description

FoodFind is a multi-device compatible web application, designed to help food-restricted users find restaurants and also specific menu items that match their dietary needs and preferences. Our team not only designed and prototyped this application from the ground up, but completed multiple user research cycles and utilized a variety of industry-specific tools to improve usability.
